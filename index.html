I'm a mechatronics engineer with a PhD in mechanical engineering, specializing in robotics. I enjoy designing mechatronic systems that make a real impact on people's lives—whether prosthetics, exoskeletons, or robots in general.

These endurance challenges remind me that we often limit ourselves by what our mind believes is possible. In reality, we're capable of far more, and many limitations simply don't exist. This mindset helps me thrive in every aspect of life—especially in the constantly evolving field of robotics.
